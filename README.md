# LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

## Objective
To analyze how top B2B SaaS operators use LinkedIn content to generate demand, build audience, and drive pipeline.

## Why this topic
LinkedIn is a primary channel for B2B SaaS where ideas are tested, distributed, and converted into business outcomes.

## Selected Experts
This research focuses on 10 B2B SaaS operators and content strategists who actively create and distribute content on LinkedIn.

## Repository Structure

- `/research/sources.md`  
  List of selected experts with links and reasoning  

- `/research/linkedin-posts/`  
  Collected LinkedIn posts organized by author  

- `/research/other/`  
  Additional notes or materials if needed  

## Goal
To extract patterns in hooks, messaging, and content formats that can be used to build a repeatable LinkedIn content strategy for B2B SaaS.
