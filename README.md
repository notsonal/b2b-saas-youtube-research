# LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

## Objective
To analyze how top B2B SaaS operators use LinkedIn content to generate demand, build audience, and influence pipeline.

## Why this topic
LinkedIn is a primary channel for B2B SaaS where founders, marketers, and operators actively share ideas, test messaging, and build authority. Understanding how content performs here is critical for modern demand generation.

## Selected Experts
This research includes 10 B2B SaaS operators and content creators known for consistently publishing high-signal LinkedIn content. They were selected based on relevance, consistency, and real-world execution rather than popularity.

## What was collected
- Curated LinkedIn posts from selected experts  
- Focus on posts that demonstrate strong hooks, clear messaging, and engagement patterns  
- Light notes on each post to highlight structure and intent  

## Repository Structure

- [sources.md](./research/sources.md)  
  List of selected experts with links, access dates, and brief annotations  

- `/research/linkedin-posts/`  
  Collected LinkedIn posts organized by author, including content and notes  

- `/research/other/`  
  Additional supporting material if needed  

## Goal
To identify repeatable patterns in hooks, positioning, and content formats that can inform a practical LinkedIn content strategy for B2B SaaS.
